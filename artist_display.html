ALI ZAFAR

Ali Zafar (Urdu: علی ظفر ‎; born 18 May 1980) is a Pakistani singer-songwriter, model, actor, producer, screenwriter and painter.[1][2] Zafar started out on Pakistani TV before becoming a popular musician.[3] He later also established a career in Bollywood and his success led many Pakistani actors to venture into Hindi films. Zafar released his second album Masty in November 2006.[5][12] The music video of the single "Dekha" was named as the most expensive music video of Pakistan, costing more than ten million rupees. The video was produced by Lux and was shot in Malaysia, featuring Reema Khan, Meera and Aaminah Haq. The song was also made part of the Hollywood film Wall Street: Money Never Sleeps, which was released in September 2010.

CAREER ACHEIVEMENTS

He has received five Lux Style Awards and a Filmfare Award nomination and recive award for song of the year in 2016. He also received Artist for Peace (Pakistan International Film Festival)and Pride of Pakistan (United For Pakistan Independence Day) in 2018 and also recive Music Icon of the Generation (Shaan-e-Pakistan Achievement in Music Awards). The same year, he collaborated with Atif Aslam for the song "Yaarian", and they performed on it on Defence Day. Zafar walken the ramp in September with Mahira Khan, showcasing the bridal collection in Divani's Bagh-e-Bahar show, at the Haveli Barood Khana.
